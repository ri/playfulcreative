Pixemo

This is an interactive installation that explores human emotions in Twitter data. Each tweet leaves a trail depending on the emotion it represents and the space becomes an visualisation of the emotional state of the current Twitterverse.

The goal of this project was to create an explorative and playful work that allows the observer to control what emotions are present in the world and also interact directly with the physical representations of the emotions.

This project was presented at the Queensland University of Technology's 2009 'This Way Up' Exhibition.

Envision

I was hired in early 2011 by a company called Ennova based in Brisbane, Australia who were developing a digital solution to construction management. Working with amazing team who were applying new thinking to an old, archaic industry, I developed the user experience of the application.

This involved firstly understanding the lives and the roles of people in this completely unfamiliar industry and witnessing their work from their perspective.

Using this information, the team and I formed stories for each of the tasks required for the client to complete their job.

From there, I designed wireframes that the design and functionality would be based on and put it all together to form the whole picture of the app.
